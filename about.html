About DD Crystal Creations

DD Crystal Creations was founded on October 18, 2023, during my final year of BCA. With a dream to become a businesswoman, I started with my pocket money and savings, crafting small resin keychains and frames for friends' birthdays.

As I experimented, I expanded into making resin clocks, resin frames, bracelets, pendants, and more. The response was overwhelming, and I started sharing my work on Instagram through reels, which helped me reach a wider audience.

Today, we have successfully completed 200+ orders with happy customers across different locations.
